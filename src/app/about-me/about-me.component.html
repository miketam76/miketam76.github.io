About Me

Sharing my coding journey, personal projects, and a little inspiration from the outdoors.

Hi, I’m Moon (Mike) Tam. My journey into software development is built on a solid foundation of years spent as an IT Support Technician, where I mastered the art of technical troubleshooting and user-focused problem-solving. I recently expanded that expertise during a 2023 - 2024 student term with the Canadian federal government, working within large-scale enterprise environments.

I like to code whenever I can squeeze it in between family life, work, and school, and this blog is where I share the projects I’ve built along the way. When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me biking along the greenbelt paths. These quiet trails inspired this blog’s forest theme and help me clear my head. My goal is to continue growing as a software developer, eventually stepping into a Senior Technical Consultant role where I can bridge the gap between complex technology and real-world user needs.
